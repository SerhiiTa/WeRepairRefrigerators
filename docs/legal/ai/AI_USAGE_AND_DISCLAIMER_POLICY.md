## 2. AI-Generated Repair Advice Disclaimer

### 2.1 Core Disclaimer

**AI-generated repair advice on this Platform is not professional advice.**

Content generated by Platform AI tools — including diagnostic suggestions, repair procedure guidance, parts recommendations, error code interpretations, and troubleshooting steps — is produced by automated systems and:

- Has not been reviewed or approved by a licensed appliance repair technician, engineer, or other qualified professional before delivery
- May not account for the specific make, model, age, condition, or modification history of the appliance in question
- May not reflect the most current manufacturer service bulletins, recalls, or updated procedures
- Is based on patterns in training data and may not be applicable to every real-world repair scenario
- Does not constitute a professional diagnosis, repair estimate, or work authorization

### 2.2 No Substitute for Professional Assessment

AI-generated repair suggestions are a starting point for investigation — not a final diagnosis or repair plan. No AI output on this Platform should be treated as a substitute for:

- Direct physical inspection of the appliance by a qualified Technician
- Consultation of the current, official manufacturer service manual
- The professional judgment of a licensed and experienced appliance repair technician
- Required safety testing following any repair

### 2.3 Appliance-Specific Limitations

AI tools may have limited or outdated knowledge of:

- Recently released appliance models and their proprietary systems
- Brand-specific diagnostic codes that differ from industry standards
- Regional or country-specific variants of appliances
- Custom or non-standard installations

## 3. AI Inaccuracies Disclaimer

### 3.1 AI Systems Make Mistakes

AI language models — including those powering Platform features — are known to produce outputs that are:

- **Factually incorrect ("hallucinations"):** AI may generate plausible-sounding but false information, including incorrect part numbers, non-existent procedures, or inaccurate technical specifications
- **Outdated:** AI training data has a knowledge cutoff date. Procedures, parts, recalls, and regulations may have changed since the AI's training
- **Incomplete:** AI may omit critical steps, safety precautions, or contextual information necessary for a safe and correct repair
- **Overconfident:** AI systems may present incorrect information with the same tone and apparent certainty as correct information
- **Context-blind:** AI may not adequately account for important context you have not explicitly provided

### 3.2 No Real-Time Data

Platform AI tools do not have access to real-time manufacturer databases, live parts availability, current recall notices, or up-to-date regulatory information unless explicitly stated and sourced.

### 3.3 Verify Before You Act

**Every AI-generated output on this Platform should be independently verified** against current, authoritative sources before being acted upon — particularly when the repair involves electrical systems, gas connections, refrigerants, sealed systems, or any component where an error could cause injury or property damage.

### 3.4 No Correction Guarantee

While the Platform actively works to improve AI accuracy, we cannot guarantee that known inaccuracies will be corrected within any specific timeframe. If you identify an AI response that is dangerously incorrect, report it immediately using the Platform's feedback tools.

## 4. Human Verification Requirement

### 4.1 Mandatory Human Judgment

All AI-generated content on this Platform — including repair advice, diagnostic suggestions, parts recommendations, community responses, translated content, and moderation decisions — requires human review and judgment before being acted upon in any professional or safety-sensitive context.

### 4.2 Technicians Must Verify

Technicians who use AI tools on this Platform to assist with diagnostics or repair planning are required to:

1. **Cross-reference AI suggestions** against the current manufacturer service manual, technical bulletins, or other authoritative sources
2. **Apply independent professional judgment** based on direct observation of the appliance
3. **Not delegate safety-critical decisions** to AI output alone
4. **Test and verify results** following any repair, regardless of whether AI guidance was consulted

See Section 10 for the full Technician verification standard.

### 4.3 Customers Must Verify

Customers who receive AI-generated information about their appliance (e.g., through a Platform chatbot or automated diagnostic tool) are responsible for:

- Not treating AI output as a professional diagnosis
- Engaging a qualified Technician before making repair or replacement decisions based on AI suggestions
- Not attempting to perform repairs based solely on AI-generated instructions

See Section 11 for the full Customer responsibility disclaimer.

### 4.4 The Platform's Verification Role

WeRepairRefrigerators does not manually review every AI-generated response before delivery. Automated filters are applied to reduce harmful outputs, but these systems are imperfect. The Platform relies on user feedback and reporting to identify and remediate problematic AI content.

## 5. No Safety Guarantee

### 5.1 Safety Disclaimer

**WeRepairRefrigerators does not guarantee that AI-generated content on this Platform is safe to act upon.**

Appliance repair involves exposure to hazards including but not limited to:

- High-voltage electrical systems
- Natural gas and propane connections
- Pressurized refrigerant systems (subject to EPA regulations)
- Sharp components and moving parts
- High-temperature surfaces and heating elements
- Water and moisture in proximity to electrical systems

AI tools on this Platform are not designed or validated as safety engineering systems. AI-generated guidance must never be treated as a substitute for:

- Proper lockout/tagout procedures
- Electrical isolation and testing protocols
- Licensed refrigerant handling procedures
- Applicable building and safety codes
- Manufacturer safety warnings and service precautions

### 5.2 No Safety Testing or Validation

The Platform has not subjected its AI systems to professional safety validation, UL certification, or any formal engineering safety review. AI outputs should be treated accordingly.

### 5.3 Stop If Unsafe

If at any point during a repair a Technician encounters a condition that appears dangerous, unexpected, or outside the scope of their training — regardless of what any AI tool has suggested — they must stop work, ensure the appliance is safely isolated, and seek qualified guidance before proceeding.

## 9. AI Moderation Limitations

- Detecting spam, abusive language, and prohibited content in Community posts
- Flagging potentially dangerous repair advice for human review
- Screening uploaded files for prohibited content
- Identifying patterns consistent with fake reviews or reputation abuse

### 9.2 Moderation Is Imperfect

AI moderation systems:

- **May produce false positives:** Removing or flagging content that does not actually violate Platform policies
- **May produce false negatives:** Failing to detect content that does violate policies

## 10. Technician Responsibility for Verification

### 10.1 Professional Standard Applies

Technicians are licensed professionals (or operate as independent contractors holding themselves out as skilled tradespeople). The use of AI tools on this Platform does not lower the professional standard of care to which Technicians are held.

### 10.2 Specific Verification Obligations

When using any Platform AI tool to assist with a repair job, Technicians must:

1. **Independently verify** all AI-generated part numbers, model compatibility information, and specifications before ordering or installing parts
2. **Cross-reference** AI-suggested repair procedures against the current official service manual for the specific appliance model
3. **Apply professional judgment** to assess whether AI-suggested procedures are appropriate for the actual condition of the appliance as observed in person
4. **Verify safety compliance** — confirm that any procedure suggested by AI complies with applicable electrical codes, EPA regulations, and manufacturer safety requirements
5. **Test the appliance** after any repair to confirm safe and correct operation, regardless of AI guidance consulted
6. **Document your process** — if a repair involves significant AI-assisted decision-making, maintain your own professional notes on the verification steps you took

### 10.3 Responsibility for Outcomes

Technicians are solely responsible for the quality, safety, and outcome of all repair work they perform, regardless of whether AI tools were consulted in the process. AI assistance does not transfer any responsibility for repair outcomes to WeRepairRefrigerators.

### 10.4 Continuing Education

AI tools are not a substitute for ongoing professional development. Technicians are encouraged to maintain current knowledge through manufacturer training, trade associations, and continuing education — not AI assistants alone.
