## 2. Independent Contractor Status

### 2.1 Contractor Relationship

You are an **independent contractor**, not an employee, agent, partner, or joint venturer of WeRepairRefrigerators. This Agreement does not create an employment relationship of any kind.

As an independent contractor, you:

- Operate your own independent appliance repair business
- Retain full control over how, when, where, and to whom you provide services
- Are free to accept or decline any job opportunity presented through the Platform
- May work for other platforms, marketplaces, or clients simultaneously (see Section 20)
- Are solely responsible for the manner and means by which you perform repair services

### 2.2 Control and Direction

WeRepairRefrigerators does not:

- Direct or supervise the performance of your repair work
- Set your work hours or require minimum availability
- Require you to wear uniforms or use Platform-branded materials
- Provide you with tools, equipment, or vehicles
- Determine which specific jobs you must accept
- Control the technical methods or procedures you use to perform repairs

### 2.3 Platform Role

The Platform's role is limited to:

- Operating the technology marketplace that connects you with Customers
- Providing profile and listing infrastructure
- Facilitating communications and payments between you and Customers
- Operating community and AI-assisted knowledge features

## 13. Fraud and Misrepresentation

### 13.1 Prohibited Conduct

The following conduct is strictly prohibited and may result in immediate account termination and legal action:

1. **Identity fraud:** Impersonating any person, creating fake accounts, or misrepresenting your identity
2. **Credential fraud:** Listing licenses, certifications, or credentials you do not hold or that are not current
3. **Insurance fraud:** Submitting false proof of insurance or misrepresenting coverage
4. **Job fraud:** Accepting payment for work not performed, or performing incomplete work and refusing to remedy it
5. **Parts fraud:** Misrepresenting parts as new, OEM, or genuine when they are not
6. **Review fraud:** Any form of review manipulation as described in Section 11.4
7. **Payment fraud:** Any attempt to defraud Customers, the Platform, or the payment processor
8. **Profile fraud:** Creating multiple accounts, acquiring fake reviews, or manipulating search rankings

### 13.2 Consequences

Confirmed fraud or misrepresentation may result in:

- Immediate permanent account termination
- Forfeiture of pending payouts
- Reporting to applicable law enforcement or licensing authorities
- Civil legal action by WeRepairRefrigerators and/or affected Customers
