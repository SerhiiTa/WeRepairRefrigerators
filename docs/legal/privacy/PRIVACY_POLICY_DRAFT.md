## 1. Introduction

WeRepairRefrigerators ("we," "us," "our," or "Platform") operates an online marketplace connecting independent appliance repair technicians ("Technicians") with homeowners and property managers ("Customers"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information about you when you use our website, mobile application, and related services (collectively, the "Platform").

By accessing or using the Platform, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Platform.

This Privacy Policy is incorporated by reference into our Terms of Service.

## 11. Public Profile Visibility

### 11.1 What Is Publicly Visible

Technician profiles on the Platform are designed to be publicly accessible and indexed by search engines. The following Technician profile information is **public by default**:

- Display name or business name
- Profile photo
- Service area (city/region level)
- Specialties and appliance types served
- Certifications and credentials (as entered by Technician)
- Average rating and number of reviews
- Review text submitted by Customers
- Response time and job completion statistics

### 11.2 What Is Not Publicly Visible

The following Technician information is **private by default** and not displayed on public profiles:

- Full home address or precise residential location
- Phone number (shared only with connected Customers)
- Tax identification information
- Government-issued ID documents
- Full earnings or financial history
- Private messages

### 11.3 Search Engine Indexing

Public profile pages may be indexed by Google, Bing, and other search engines. Removing your account does not guarantee immediate removal from search engine caches. We will submit de-indexing requests upon account deletion, but cannot control third-party search engine behavior.

### 11.4 Customer Job Postings

Customer job postings may be partially visible to the public (e.g., appliance type and general area) to attract Technician interest. Full Customer contact details are not displayed publicly.
