## 1. Platform Overview

WeRepairRefrigerators (referred to herein as "the Platform," "we," "us," or "our") is an online marketplace that connects independent appliance repair technicians ("Technicians") with homeowners and property managers ("Customers") seeking appliance repair services. The Platform operates as a technology intermediary and does not itself provide repair services.

The Platform currently offers or plans to offer the following features:

- **Public SEO Marketplace:** A publicly accessible directory of available repair jobs and Technician listings, indexed for search engine visibility.
- **Technician Profiles:** Public-facing profiles showcasing Technician credentials, service areas, specialties, reviews, and availability.
- **Open Jobs Marketplace:** A listing board where Customers can post repair requests and Technicians can express interest or bid.
- **AI-Assisted Community:** An artificial intelligence–powered community and knowledge hub designed to assist Technicians with technical guidance, diagnostics, and peer discussion.
- **Payment Processing:** Future integration with Stripe or similar third-party processors to facilitate payments between Customers and Technicians. `[ATTORNEY REVIEW REQUIRED: confirm payment flow and regulatory compliance]`
- **Subscription Services:** Planned subscription tiers for Technicians and/or Customers offering premium features. `[PLACEHOLDER — see Section 18]`

The Platform is intended for use within the United States and is designed to support future nationwide expansion.
